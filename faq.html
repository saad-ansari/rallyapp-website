Frequently Asked Questions

What is Rally?

Rally is a social accountability app that tracks who actually shows up to events. Create squads with your friends, plan events, and build your Reliability Score based on your attendance.

What is a Reliability Score?

Your Reliability Score is a measure of how often you follow through on your commitments. When you RSVP "Yes" to an event and show up, your score goes up. If you bail without notice, it goes down. It's a fun way to see who in your friend group is truly reliable.

How is my Reliability Score calculated?

Your score is based on:

  • Events you RSVP "Yes" to
  • Whether you checked in or attended those events
  • How often and when you cancel after committing

Early cancellations have less impact than last-minute bail-outs. The more events you attend, the more accurate your score becomes.

Who can see my Reliability Score?

Your score is only visible to members of your squads and hosts of events you RSVP to. It is not publicly visible to anyone outside your squads.

What is a Squad?

A Squad is a group of friends or people who regularly hang out together. You can create squads for different friend groups, sports teams, book clubs, or any recurring gathering. Each squad has its own events and leaderboard.

How do I check in to an event?

When you arrive at an event location, open Rally and tap the check-in button. The app uses your location to verify you're actually at the venue. This prevents fake check-ins and keeps scores honest.

What if I can't make an event after RSVPing?

Life happens! If you need to cancel, do it as early as possible through the app. Early cancellations have minimal impact on your score. Last-minute cancellations or no-shows will affect your Reliability Score more significantly.

Is Rally free to use?

Yes, Rally is free to download and use. We may introduce optional premium features in the future, but the core functionality will always be free.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from within the app by going to Settings > Account > Delete Account. You can also request account deletion by emailing support@rallyapp.me.

How do I report a problem or get help?

For support, you can reach us at support@rallyapp.me or use the in-app help feature under Settings > Help & Support.

Is my data safe?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We only collect the information necessary to provide the service. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

What age do I need to be to use Rally?

You must be at least 13 years old to use Rally. Users under 13 are not permitted to create accounts.
