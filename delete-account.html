Delete Account

Request deletion of your Rally account

We're sorry to see you go. If you wish to delete your Rally account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

Warning: This action is permanent

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, you will not be able to recover any of your data, including your reliability score, event history, or squad memberships.

What Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

Data that will be deleted:
  • Your profile and personal information
  • Your squad memberships
  • Event history and RSVPs
  • Your reliability score and achievements
  • Photos you've uploaded
  • Notification history

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account, you can use one of the following methods:

Option 1: Through the App

Open the Rally app, go to Settings > Account > Delete Account and follow the prompts.

Option 2: Contact Support

Send an email to our support team requesting account deletion. Please include the email address associated with your Rally account.

Contact Support

Send an email with your account deletion request

support@rallyapp.me

Processing Time

Within 14 Days

Your account deletion request will be processed within 14 days of receipt. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been permanently deleted.

Questions?

If you have any questions about account deletion or data privacy, please refer to our Privacy Policy or contact us at support@rallyapp.me.
