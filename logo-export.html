Rally Logo Export

Below is the 1024x1024 logo for app icons. Use your browser's screenshot tool or DevTools to export.

How to export:

  1. Right-click on the large logo below
  2. Select Inspect (or press F12)
  3. Right-click the element in DevTools
  4. Choose Capture node screenshot
  5. This saves a perfect 1024x1024 PNG!
