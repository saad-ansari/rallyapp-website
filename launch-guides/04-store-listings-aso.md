## What Is ASO (App Store Optimization)?

ASO is **SEO for app stores**. It's how people discover Rally when searching "reliability app", "squad planner", "friend tracker", etc.

**Goal:** Rank in top 10 for target keywords ‚Üí Get organic downloads without ads.

**Two components:**
1. **Keyword Research** - What people search for
2. **Store Listing** - How Rally appears in search results

**3. Manual Method (100% Free):**
- Open App Store ‚Üí Search "reliability"
- Notice autocomplete suggestions (these are high-volume searches)
- Screenshot the dropdown - these are your keywords!

**Example findings:**
