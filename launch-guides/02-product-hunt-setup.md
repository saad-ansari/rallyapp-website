## Why Product Hunt Matters

Product Hunt can drive **1,000-5,000+ downloads** on launch day if done right. But the algorithm heavily weights upvotes from **active community members**. If you create an account the day before launch, your upvotes won't count much.

**The 2-Month Rule:** Engage for 2-3 months before launching = your network reciprocates on launch day.

**Rally's Timeline:** Start today (Jan 17) ‚Üí Launch Week 8 (early March) = 7 weeks of engagement. Tight, but doable.

## Part 2: Daily Engagement Strategy (Weeks 1-7)

### The Daily Routine (10-15 mins/day)

**Every day at the same time** (e.g., morning coffee, lunch break):

**1. Go to:** [producthunt.com](https://producthunt.com)

**2. Browse Today's Launches:**
- Scroll through 5-10 products
- Read descriptions, click through to websites
- **Pick 5 products** you genuinely find interesting

**3. Upvote 5-10 Products:**
- Click the "‚ñ≥ Upvote" button
- **Don't upvote everything** - PH tracks this, looks spammy
- **Only upvote** if you'd actually use it or it's well-made

**4. Leave 2-3 Thoughtful Comments:**
- **NOT:** "Great app! Good luck üöÄ" (generic, low value)
- **YES:** Ask questions, share feedback, mention use case

**Example Good Comments:**

‚ùå **Bad:** "Looks great! Upvoted üëç"

‚úÖ **Good:** "Love the minimalist UI! Quick question - how do you handle timezone differences for remote teams? That's been our biggest pain point with [competing product]. Upvoted!"

‚úÖ **Good:** "This is exactly what I've been looking for! One suggestion: would be great to have Slack integration for notifications. Are you planning that?"

‚úÖ **Good:** "Congrats on the launch! I run a 10-person squad that struggles with coordination. Does Rally work for larger groups, or is it optimized for smaller friend circles?"

**Why Good Comments Work:**
- Shows you actually used/understood the product
- Asks thoughtful question (maker will reply ‚Üí conversation)
- Mentions pain point or use case (relatable)
- Ends with upvote mention (positive reinforcement)

**5. Engage with Replies:**
- If maker replies to your comment, respond back
- Build relationship, not just transactional engagement

**6. Optional: Share on Twitter**
- Tweet 1-2 PH products you loved
- Tag the maker, use #ProductHunt
- Builds goodwill (makers remember this)

**Should you use it?** YES - it's free traffic and builds anticipation.

## Part 4: What NOT to Do

### Common Product Hunt Mistakes:

‚ùå **Creating account day before launch**  
- Algorithm won't weight your network's upvotes
- Looks like coordinated vote manipulation

‚ùå **Only engaging on launch day**  
- "Hey, I launched! Please upvote!" = ignored
- Community spots this instantly

‚ùå **Generic comments**  
- "Great app! Good luck!" x100 = spam
- No value, no relationships built

‚ùå **Upvoting everything**  
- Product Hunt tracks this
- Flags as low-quality engagement

‚ùå **Asking for upvotes directly**  
- Violates PH guidelines
- Can get your launch penalized
- **Instead:** "I'd love your feedback" (implied ask)

‚ùå **Ignoring comments on your launch**  
- Algorithm tracks maker responsiveness
- Not replying = deprioritized

## Part 5: Building Your Launch Network

### Who to Connect With

**Weeks 1-3:** Follow makers who launch products similar to Rally:
- Social networking apps
- Productivity tools
- Community/group coordination apps

**How to find them:**
- Search Product Hunt for "social app," "productivity," "planning"
- Follow makers of products you genuinely like
- Engage with their launches (comment, upvote)

**Weeks 4-6:** Engage with makers who engage back:
- If someone replies to your comments, follow them
- Like their responses, continue conversation
- Build genuine relationships

**Week 7:** DM top relationships:
- "Hey [Name], loved your launch of [Product]. Rally is launching next week - would love your feedback when we go live!"
- **Don't explicitly ask for upvotes** - just invite feedback
- Many will upvote organically
