Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account through the app (Settings → Account → Delete Account) or by emailing us at support@rallyapp.me. Learn more on our Delete Account page.

How is my Reliability Score calculated?

Your Reliability Score is based on your attendance patterns, including RSVPs, check-ins, and cancellations. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

Can I export my data?

Yes! You can request a data export by emailing support@rallyapp.me with the subject "Data Export Request." We'll provide your data within 30 days.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Please email us at support@rallyapp.me with details about the bug, including your device type, OS version, and steps to reproduce the issue.
